A Letter About Love

Love Poem

True love transcends language

And it is not bound to the constructs of our reality.

True love is unconditional

Not dependent upon circumstances or time.

But rather it is a state of being in the soul

Nurtured and cultivated by the creator.

True love is when you look inside one's eyes and feel your souls were meant to collide.

True love is filled with passion and grace for another's mistake.

True love is like an ocean that has many depths to be explored.

And, the beauty in it is worth more than what can be seen from shore.

True love is enamored with another's being mind, body, and spirit.

It is cultivated by one's presence. It is inspired by one's intellect

True love is illuminating.

It has the power to shine so bright it lights the dimmest part of one's heart.

If you are so fortunate to find true love, you will be in awe at the millions of ways it engulfs your soul.

True love has the ability to be the embodiment of everything that feels right to you.

True love is understanding beyond what words can utter.

And, true love not only sees you for who you are, but for all you can be.

True love may not be perfect, but true love is everything and all you need.

By: Imani Vera Walker
